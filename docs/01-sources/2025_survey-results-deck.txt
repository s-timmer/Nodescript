NodeScript Survey Results 2025 — slide deck (12 pages). Source: PDF upload.

Survey of NodeScript signups to understand activation patterns.

Key metrics:
- 436 total signups analyzed
- ~30% activation rate among surveyed users (improved from earlier 8% figure)
- Drop-off reasons quantified

Top reasons for not activating:
- "Didn't have time to explore" — 41%
- "Didn't find what I needed" — 29%
- "Too complicated to get started" — 18%
- Other / unclear — 12%

Recommendations from survey analysis:
1. Quick Win tutorials — #1 priority recommendation
2. Better first-run experience with guided paths
3. Template library for common use cases
4. Improved documentation discoverability

User feedback themes:
- Positive: power, flexibility, instant deployment
- Negative: steep learning curve, unclear starting point, documentation gaps
- Users who persisted found significant value

Survey methodology:
- Email outreach to registered users
- Mix of activated and non-activated users
- Qualitative + quantitative questions
